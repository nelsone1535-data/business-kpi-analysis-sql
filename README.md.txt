
## Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data to evaluate key business performance metrics such as profitability, sales performance, and customer segment value.

The goal of this project is to simulate the type of analysis a data analyst might perform to help a business understand revenue drivers, profit margins, and the impact of discounts on profitability.

The analysis focuses on identifying:

* Profit margins by product category
* The relationship between discounts and profitability
* High value customer segments
* Profitability classification of transactions

---

## Tools Used

* PostgreSQL
* SQL
* Data Analysis

---

## Dataset

This project uses a retail sales dataset commonly known as the "Superstore Dataset".
The dataset includes information about:

* product categories
* customer segments
* geographic regions
* sales revenue
* discounts
* profits

---

## Business Questions Answered

1. What are the profit margins across product categories?
2. Are discounts negatively impacting profit?
3. Which customer segments generate the most value?
4. Which transactions generate high profit versus losses?
