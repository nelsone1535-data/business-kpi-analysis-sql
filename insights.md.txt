1. Profit Margin by Category

Some product categories generate significantly higher profit margins than others. Understanding which categories produce the highest margins can help businesses prioritize inventory and marketing efforts.

2. Discount Impact on Profitability

Higher discount levels are associated with lower average profit per order, suggesting that aggressive discounting strategies may reduce overall profitability.

3. Transaction Profitability

Individual transactions vary widely in profitability. Classifying transactions as high profit, moderate profit, or loss-making helps identify areas where pricing or discount policies may need adjustment.

4. Customer Segment Value

Customer segments contribute differently to total sales and profit. Identifying the most valuable customer segments allows businesses to focus marketing efforts on the most profitable audiences.
